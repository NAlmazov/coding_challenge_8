Smart Calendar

FOXI tracks the availability of all of your family members. You can optionally designate members of your family as "Master Organizer".
FOXI's smart calendar has privacy settings, which allows your family to always have privacy.
FOXI periodically checks everyones calendar and suggested slots that can be used for family activites!





Activity Suggestion

FOXI stays up to date on all new fun games, activites, and events. You can persoanlize recommendations to fit your family personality by answering a short questionnaire!
FOXI is connected to all major event platforms, allowing your family to purchase or reserve seats at all of the events happening near you!
FOXI is used by so many families! You can set up a family profile and connect with other families for more fun!

Easy-Chat™

FOXI easily pulls existing conversations out of your SMS messenger, Facebook Messenger, and other popular platfroms.
FOXI's chat interface is extremely easy to use and allows you to create your own "family" emoji's and GIF's.
FOXI allows to keep all important family conversations in one place!

To-Do List

FOXI makes it extremely easy to track what your family needs to do!
FOXI's unique bounty system allows everyone to incentivize others to complete the tasks!
FOXI keeps track of who likes to do what, which makes it a lot easier to assign chores.
